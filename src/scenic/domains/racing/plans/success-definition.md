# Success Definition

## Minimum Success

- ego no longer blindly drives nominal line into a slower opponent
- ego chooses `left` / `right` / `optimal` intelligently
- ego follows safely and repositions without chatter

## Strong Success

- ego can complete and abort overtakes safely
- decisions are stable and segment-aware
- traffic performance is clearly better than nominal-line baseline

## Full Smart-Driving Success

- ego handles multi-car traffic robustly over long runs
- tactics improve both safety and race performance
- planner maturity makes pit integration the next step, not a rewrite
